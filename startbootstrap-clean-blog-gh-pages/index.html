Brief Biography

I was born in Murray, Utah, and moved to Bentonville, Arkansas when I was nine years old. Growing up in the South instilled in me a profound love for community and the outdoors. At 19, I moved to Southern Arizona and learned Spanish as I served the latino community with my local congregation from The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. After completing 16 months in AZ, I moved back up to Utah to begin my undergraduate career at Brigham Young University, studying Spanish and Business. Provo is where I currently reside, as I prepare to apply to the Human Resource Management program in BYU's Marriott School of Business.
